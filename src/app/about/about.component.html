American Dodgeball Association Of America (ADAA) is pleased to announce the annual tournament to be held at the Las Vegas Learning Annex. This year it's being broadcast on ESPN 8: The Ocho, "If it's almost a sport, it's on The Ocho". The winning team will be awarded the grand prize of $50,000.

Teams must win the the sub-regional qualifying game to enter the Tournament.

"Just remember the five D's of dodgeball: Dodge, Duck, Dip, Dive and... Dodge."
